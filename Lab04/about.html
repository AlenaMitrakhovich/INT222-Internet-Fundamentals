About Me

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my site. My name is Alena and I am a Computer Programming student at Seneca College. You may be wondering how and why I chose this path and here is a short summary of how I got here.

I began my post-secondary education at York University where I completed a 4 year degree in Business from which I graduated in 2013. Post graduation, I found a full-time job with Randstad Canada as a Resource Manager. Essentially, I was a recruiter/head-hunter specializing in the IT industry. I worked with many companies and placed many IT professionals in many different roles. Although I enjoyed this position, I quickly realized that I wanted to do something that was more challenging and interesting. Through many conversations over a 2 year span with my clients, I came to the decision that I wanted to become a Computer Programmer myself. Hence, after 2 years with Randstad, I resigned and came back to school to learn Computer Programming.

Stay stuned as I make progress and continue updating this website as I learn more and become a more capable UI developer :)
